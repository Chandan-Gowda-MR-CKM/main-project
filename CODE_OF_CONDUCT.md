**Code of Conduct**



To maintain a safe and positive environment, all contributors must follow these rules:



**Be Respectful**

1. Treat everyone with kindness

2\. Avoid rude or offensive language



**Be Collaborative**

1.Help others

2\. Share knowledge

3\. Be open to feedback



**No Harassment**

1. No discrimination

2\. No bullying

3\. No personal attacks



**Follow Project Rules**

1. Follow contribution guidelines

2\. Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainer
